# AI Engineer — (example lab)

## About the role

Join our applied team building products on top of frontier language models. You
will fine-tune models, build evaluation harnesses, and ship LLM-powered features.

## Responsibilities

- Fine-tune and adapt large language models for specific tasks.
- Build evaluation pipelines to measure model quality and regressions.
- Design prompts and agentic workflows that use tools and retrieval.
- Run distributed training and manage experiments.

## Requirements

- Excellent Python skills.
- Practical experience with PyTorch and the Hugging Face ecosystem.
- Experience with LLM fine-tuning (LoRA, full fine-tuning, or RLHF).
- Understanding of distributed training and large-scale experimentation.
- Familiarity with prompt engineering and building LLM applications.
- Nice to have: MLOps, experiment tracking (Weights & Biases), vector databases.
